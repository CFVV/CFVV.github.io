En curso2025 → · PUC × CIGIDEN

Anticipating informal settlement growth from satellite imagery

Machine-learning models over multi-year satellite time series to detect and forecast the emergence and expansion of informal settlements in Greater Valparaíso.

Question
Can satellite time series anticipate where informal settlements will emerge, early enough for planning and risk management to act?
Material
Sentinel-2 and Esri Wayback imagery, labeled settlement polygons (2018–2025), reproducible Python pipelines.
Output
Predictive model + exposure layer for the SIRVAL territorial risk platform.

En curso2025 → · IMFD, Cities Group

Urban visual elements and geolocated crime

Which predefined visual elements of the streetscape predict or correlate with crime? Building labeled datasets and testing segmentation and object-detection models on Santiago street-view imagery.

Question
Do measurable features of the built environment carry a signal about where crime concentrates — and which ones?
Material
Street-view imagery of Santiago, geolocated crime records, annotation protocols, segmentation and detection models.
Output
Labeled dataset + empirical analysis (paper in preparation).
